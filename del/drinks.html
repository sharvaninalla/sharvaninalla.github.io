DRINKS AND BEVERAGES

CLASSIC LEMONADE

INGREDIENTS:
6 lemons
1 cup white sugar
6 cups water, or more as needed

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Juice lemons; you should have 1 cup juice.
2. Combine juice, sugar, and water in a 1/2-gallon pitcher. Stir until sugar dissolves. Taste and add more water if desired.
3. Chill and serve over ice.



MINT CUCUMBER COOLER

INGREDIENTS:
50g cucumber, sliced, plus an additional few slices for garnish
5 mint leaves, plus extra for garnish
1 tsp powdered sweetener
2 tbsp lemon juice, freshly squeezed
200ml soda water / club soda
Ice


INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Combine the cucumber, mint, lemon juice and sweetener (see note 1) and blend using a stick / immersion blender.
2. Fill a glass half full with ice cubes (see note 2). Pour over the cucumber mix.
3. Top the glass with soda water and swirl to combine using a cocktail mixer (or just a straw). Delicious!

POMEGRANATE VIRGIN MARGARITA

INGREDIENTS:
1 cup limeade (mixed limeade, can be from concentrate, just mix it with water before use)
1/4 cup simple syrup
2 TBL fresh lime juice
1/3 - 1/2 cup pomegranate juice
club soda
basil
lime wedges
pomegranate seeds for garnish
sugar and salt for the rim
ice

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Slice your lime and run lime wedge around edge of glasses
2. Dip the rim of your glass into the sugar and salt mixture, set aside
3. Mix together limeade, simple syrup, pomegranate juice, and lime juice.
4. Fill glasses with ice (about 1/3 - 1/2 full)
5. Pour mixture evenly into both glasses.
6. Top with club soda.
7. Add lime wedge, pomegranate seeds, and basil leaf.
8. Plunge basil leaf into drink and squeeze lime wedge into it before drinking.

BLUE LAGOON 

INGREDIENTS:
Blue Curacao Syrup – 40 ml
Fresh Lemon Juice – 1/2 tablespoon
Simple Sugar Syrup – 1.5 tablespoon or as per your taste
Lemonade – 200 ml – You can use sprite or 7Up.
Soda – 60 ml
Lemon wedges for garnishing (Optional)

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Take a tall glass and add blue curaçao syrup, simple sugar syrup and lemon juice to it. 
2. Now add 1-2 lemon wedges and fill half of the glass with ice.
3. Now its times to add lemonade, following by topping it with the soda.
4. Stir lightly with a spoon and garnish with lemon wedges or any other gently bitter product, such as lime.

STRAWBERRY MILKSHAKE

INGREDIENTS:
6-8 (fresh or frozen) Ripe Strawberries (approx. 1 cup halved Strawberries)
1/2 cup Milk
1/2 cup Ice Cream (Vanilla or Strawberry)
1 teaspoon Honey or Sugar, optional.

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Wash the strawberries and pat dry them. Remove stems and cut into halves.
2. Add milk, ice cream and halved strawberries in a blender jar. If you like, you can add 1 teaspoon sugar or honey for extra sweetness.
3. Blend until smooth puree. Pour it into serving glasses. Garnish with strawberry and serve.
4. Savor its flavorful taste and creamy texture as breakfast drink or party drink.

CAPPUCCINO

INGREDIENTS:
For cappuccino mixture:
1/4 cup instant coffee powder
1/4 cup sugar
3 tbsp water
Other ingredient:
Milk (as required)

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Firstly, in a large bowl take ¼ cup instant coffee powder, ¼ cup sugar and 3 tbsp water.
2. Mix well dissolving sugar.
3. Using hand beater beat the mixture. you can also beat using whisk or blender.
4. Beat for 5 minutes or until the mixture turns frothy and lightens in colour.
5. Make sure the mixture is thick and creamy. you can store this mixture for a week in an airtight container by refrigerating.
6. To prepare cappuccino, heat 2 cups of milk.
7. Whisk and get the milk to boil to make the milk frothy.
8. In a cup, add 1 tbsp of cappuccino mixture.
9. Top with a cup of frothy milk and mix gently.
10. To make it stronger, add a tbsp of more cappuccino mixture and mix gently.
11. Finally, enjoy cappuccino hot with cookies.
